Delicious Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, paprika, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  3. Rub the mixture over the chicken breasts and let them marinate for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Place the chicken breasts on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes until cooked through.
  5. Once done, sprinkle with fresh parsley before serving.
