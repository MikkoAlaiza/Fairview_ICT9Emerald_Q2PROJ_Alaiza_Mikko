Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 slice of tomato
  • 1 leaf of lettuce
  • 2 slices of ham or turkey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Layer the cheese, tomato, lettuce, and ham (if using) on one slice of bread.
  3. Top with the other slice of bread, mayo side facing down.
  4. Cut the sandwich in half and serve immediately with a side of chips or salad.
