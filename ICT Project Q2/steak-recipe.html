Perfectly Grilled Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 steaks (ribeye or sirloin)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (like thyme or rosemary) for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush the steaks with olive oil and season generously with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill the steaks for 4-5 minutes per side (for medium-rare) or until your desired doneness.
  4. Optional: During the last minute of grilling, place a small amount of butter on each steak for added flavor.
  5. Remove from heat and let the steaks rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  6. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve with your favorite sides.
