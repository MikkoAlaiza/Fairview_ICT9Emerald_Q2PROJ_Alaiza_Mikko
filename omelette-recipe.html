Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional fillings: cheese, mushrooms, spinach, onions, tomatoes, ham

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them with a fork until well mixed.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and add butter.
  3. Once the butter is melted and foamy, pour in the beaten eggs and let them set for 30 seconds.
  4. As the eggs begin to set, use a spatula to lift the edges and let the uncooked egg flow underneath.
  5. Add your fillings on one half of the omelette.
  6. Once the omelette is mostly set, fold it in half and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until fully cooked through.
  7. Serve warm with a side of toast or salad.
