Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (or almond milk for a dairy-free option)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place the banana, berries, yogurt, and honey (or syrup) into a blender.
  2. Add ice cubes for a thicker consistency, if desired.
  3. Blend until smooth, adding more liquid if necessary.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately!
